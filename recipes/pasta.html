Pasta Pesto Chicken

Description:

A simple protein rich and high carb dish, perfect as a post-workout meal.

No diary, made with pesto from basil and cashews and topped with soya cooking cream.

Ingredients:

  • 100g fusili pasta
  • 100g chicken breast
  • 100g broccoli
  • 1-2 tbsp. vegan green pesto
  • 30ml soy cooking cream
  • pinch black pepper
  • pinch salt (optional - pesto is salty)

Steps:

  1. Salt the chicken breast and cook it for 10-15min.
  2. Cook the broccoli for 10min.
  3. Boil the pasta for 10-12min. and do not throw all the water away when ready
  4. Slice the chicken and broccoli into pieces
  5. Put all together in a pan with 1 tbsp. pasta water (low fire)
  6. Add pepper & salt
  7. Put the pesto and stir for 1-2min.
  8. Set away from the fire and top it up with soy cream
