Pumpkin & Sweet Potato Soup

Description:

A very nutritious, high fiber soup, perfect as a side or even main dish.

Ingredients:

  • 400g pumpkin (also good frozen chopped one)
  • 3 medium sweet potatoes
  • chestnuts (cooked and vacuumed)
  • 100g spinach
  • 30g butter or 3 tbsp. olive oil
  • 3 tsp. veggie stock powder
  • soy cooking cream
  • 1 tsp. salt

Steps:

  1. Slice the potatoes
  2. Put together - potatoes, pumpkin, stock, butter and salt in a pot, pour water until all covered
  3. Let it boil for 10-13min.
  4. Set away from the fire and pour a coup for 1 person
  5. Top it up with handful spinach (20g), chestnuts and soy cream
