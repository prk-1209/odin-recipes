Salmon & Edamame

Description:

A simple omega3, protein rich and high fiber meal, perfect as lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 130g salmon (fresh or canned)
  • 100g edamame
  • 50g basmati rice
  • 1-2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. apple vinegar
  • 1 tsp. honey
  • 1 tsp. chilli sauce (optional)

Steps:

  1. Salt the salmon and cook it for 10min. if fresh
  2. Prepare a sauce in a cup - soy sauce, vinegar, honey, chilli and garlic
  3. Cook the edamame in the sauce for 1-2min.
  4. Cook the rice aside for 10-12min. (optional: rice is better for gut health when cooked, refrigerated afterwards)
  5. Serve and top it with nuts or seeds of choice and add extra chilli sauce if wanted
